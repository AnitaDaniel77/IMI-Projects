Little Lemon — Usability Test Script

Menu Item Page

Introduction

Hi, my name is Anita. Thank you for your time today. We are testing the design, not you — there are no wrong answers. May I record this session for internal use only? Please sign the consent form before we begin.

Warm-up Questions

  • Could you introduce yourself?
  • What do you do for work?
  • What mobile device do you use most?
  • How often do you order food online?

Screen Tour

Take a moment to look at this screen without tapping anything.

  • What do you think this screen is for?
  • What is the first thing you notice?

Tasks

Please think aloud as you complete each task.

  • Task 1: You want to order one Greek salad. How would you do that?
  • Task 2: You want to add extra feta cheese. Can you show me how?
  • Task 3: You want to check your basket. Where would you go?
  • Task 4: You changed your mind and want to go back to the menu. What would you do?

Wrap-up Questions

  • How easy or difficult was that — 1 to 5?
  • Was anything unclear or confusing?
  • What did you expect to happen when you tapped that?

Post-Test Interview

  • How would you rate your overall experience?
  • Was anything missing?
  • What would you change?
  • Do you have any questions for me?

Thank you so much for your time and feedback today.
